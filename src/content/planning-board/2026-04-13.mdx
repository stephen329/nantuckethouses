## Meeting Highlights

Monday's Planning Board session was dominated by the workforce housing discussion, which ran over two hours with significant public comment. The commercial signage item was a quick approval.

### Workforce Housing Public Hearing

The proposed subdivision on Old South Road would create 24 workforce housing units on a 4.5-acre parcel currently zoned LUG-2. The developer is seeking a density bonus under the town's inclusionary zoning provisions.

Key points from the discussion:

- **Traffic**: Abutters raised concerns about additional vehicle trips on Old South Road, particularly during summer months
- **Density**: Board members questioned whether 24 units was appropriate given the surrounding single-family character
- **Design**: The board responded positively to the cottage-cluster design approach but wants more detail on building heights and spacing

The hearing was continued to the next meeting pending additional traffic analysis.

### LUG-2 Ground Cover Update

The board previewed a proposed amendment to ground cover calculations in LUG-2 districts. The key change would include permeable pavers in the ground cover calculation at a 50% rate (currently excluded). This could significantly impact buildable area on many Mid-Island lots.
